    Cloud Native Strategy

    We don’t invest capital in data centers and hardware, nor do we sell incumbent solutions and maintain datacenters. Instead, we bring leading edge technology to life through Cloud. Cloud computing refers to the on-demand delivery of IT resources and applications via the internet with pay as you go prices.

    100% AWS Certified Architects & Engineers

    Cloud House ensures that all of our architects and engineers are AWS Certified, and lead in every aspect of this transformative technology to enable the enterprise to innovate and lead their industry by using Cloud.

    At Cloud House, our company was born in the Cloud era where our business values, processes and employees have been groomed from a Cloud Native Strategy.

    Across execution, we focus on the following Cloud Native Principles;

    • Trade capital expense for operating expense
    • Benefit from massive economies of scale
    • Stop guessing capacity
    • Increase speed and agility
    • Outsource non-core activities to reduce requirements for skills in-house & focus on core business
    • Go global in minutes
    • 100% AWS Certified Architects & Engineers

    Secure by Default

    One of the core fundamentals of Cloud House is our focus on Secure AWS Infrastructure by Default. We implement best practice security architectures for the Cloud which align with the AWS ‘Shared Responsibility Model’ and implement Tier 3+ secure systems as a default component of using our expertise.

    Being Secure by Default means;

    • Infrastructure configured with Idempotent security patterns
    • Multi factor authentication tokens stored in a safety deposit box with annual rotation
    • Encryption key annual rotation
    • Encryption in transit and at rest where necessary
    • Web application firewalls and unified threat management across all infrastructure
    • Subnet segregation and best practice implementation
    • Pro-active threat scanning, authentication monitoring and change control
    • All API’s, Logs and AWS access recorded and proactively monitored with alerting
    • Quarterly security audits
    • Best practice identity & access management
    • Automation is implemented meaning no human intervention

    Design for Failure

    Mission critical is a mindset - we think differently and look around corners to head off problems other people haven’t even dreamed of. Failure costs time and opportunity as well as money. That’s why Cloud House design with the expectation that things can break.

    Always Available

    We build Cloud infrastructure that is always Highly Available, coupled with automation to be self-healing and fault-tolerant. Leveraging the dozens of AWS data centers and regions, we build distributed systems that simply, just work.

    We take the headache out of complex management and overheads that High Availability can create, and warrant “Guaranteed Uptime” through our managed services program.

    What does Always Available mean?

    • 99.99% uptime
    • Self-healing through immutable infrastructure
    • Mitigation from isolated datacenter issues
    • Distribution across multiple datacenters that are geographically independent
    • Business Continuity assurance
