    Cloud Assessment

    Understanding your IT environment and how this translates to Cloud is a fundamental first step towards a successful Cloud Migration Strategy. Our Cloud Assessment creates an iterative Cloud migration roadmap for your environment and identifies the benefits and approach to migrating workloads and infrastructure to a desired state based on AWS.

    Your Trusted Advisor for Cloud Migration

    We provide a comprehensive analysis of your current state, and build a future state roadmap & migration strategy incorporating Total Cost of Ownership, ROI duration, best practice implementation, best practice security design and formulate an iterative roadmap for your organisation to adopt a desired Cloud strategy and environment.

    Cloud Readiness Assessment Covers

    • Current state design and High Level Overview
    • Future state design and High Level Overview
    • Best practice security overview
    • Total Cost of Ownership & Net Present Value
    • Return on Investment
    • Cloud migration roadmap & strategy
    • Operational model
